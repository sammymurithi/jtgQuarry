Who we are

JTG Aggregates Quarry is Thika's leading supplier of high-quality construction materials, serving construction companies, real estate developers, and contractors across Kenya.

With years of experience in the aggregates industry, we provide reliable supply, competitive pricing, and fast delivery of ballast, sand, gravel, and other construction materials. Our commitment to quality and customer satisfaction has made us the trusted partner for major construction projects in Thika and surrounding areas.
