Who we are

JTG Aggregates Quarry was established in 2014 as a leading supplier of high-quality construction materials in Thika, Kenya. Our mission is to provide reliable, sustainable, and cost-effective construction aggregates to support the growing infrastructure development in Kenya.

With over a decade of experience in the aggregates industry, we have built a reputation for excellence in quality, reliability, and customer service. Our quarry operations are strategically located in Thika, serving construction companies, real estate developers, and contractors across Central Kenya and Nairobi Metropolitan area.
